Atomic Habit Main Theme


Until you make the unconscious conscious,it will direct your life and you will call it fate.
The first step to change bad habits is to be on the lookout for them.
One of the most practical ways to eliminate a bad habit is to reduce exposure to the cue that causes it.
The Two-minute rule states,When you start a new habit it should take less than two minutes to do.
Always give short term reward after completing a particular habit.
To make a habit satisfying create a to do list and check it after completing a particular habit.
